Tips

Nail Care & Aftercare

  • Avoid soaking nails in hot water for the first 24 hours.
  • Do not use your nails to open cans or scratch hard surfaces.
  • Be careful with oils and spices that may stain light coloured nails.
  • Clean underneath nails gently with mild soap and a soft brush.
  • Book refill appointments regularly to maintain nail strength.
